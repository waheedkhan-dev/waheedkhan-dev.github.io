2. Description of Service

Routinic is an Android habit tracker that helps you build and maintain positive daily routines. The app provides:

  • Create and manage daily, weekly, monthly, and one-time habits using custom settings or pre-built templates
  • Track habit completion, build streaks, and earn Awesome Days
  • View progress through bar-chart analytics, a monthly calendar heatmap, and per-habit statistics
  • Set up to three reminders per habit with exact-time alarms
  • Run timed habits using the built-in countdown timer
  • Break bad habits using the dedicated Quit Bad Habits category
  • Customise the app with Light, Dark, and Dark Blue themes

This data is not linked to your identity and is used solely for push notifications and app stability.

  • Data loss risk: If you uninstall Routinic or clear app data, your local habit history is permanently deleted from your device. We cannot recover it.

  • Firebase services: We use Firebase Firestore (device installation data), Firebase Crashlytics (crash reports), and Firebase Analytics (anonymous usage data) to operate and improve the app. None of these services receive your personal habit data.

6. Reminders & Notifications

Routinic uses Android's exact alarm system to deliver habit reminders at the times you configure. By setting reminders, you grant Routinic permission to schedule and deliver local notifications on your device.

  • Reminders are delivered locally and do not require an internet connection.
  • Disable reminders anytime in Android Settings → Apps → Routinic → Notifications.
  • Routinic reschedules your reminders automatically after your device restarts.
  • If reminders are unreliable, check that Routinic is not restricted by Android battery optimisation settings.
