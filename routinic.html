Features

Everything you need,
nothing you don't.

Routinic is built around one idea: the fewer decisions you need to make, the more energy you have to actually do the habit.

📅

Daily Habit Dashboard

See all your habits for today in one clear view with a weekly calendar strip. Check off habits with a single tap.

🏆

Streak Tracking

Track your current streak, best streak, and total completions per habit. Every day you show up counts.

📊

Progress Statistics

View Awesome Days, completion history, and per-habit analytics with a monthly calendar heatmap.

😊

Mood Tracking

Log your daily mood with notes. See weekly mood charts and understand how habits affect how you feel.

⏱️

Built-in Timer

Timed habits like meditation or workouts use a built-in countdown timer. Start, pause, and complete — all in one place.

🔔

Smart Notifications

Set per-habit reminders, get a daily plan summary, and receive a gentle end-of-day progress report.

🎨

4 Beautiful Themes

Choose from System, Light, Dark, or Dark Blue. The app adapts to the look that fits your mood.

🗂️

Habit Categories

Pick from curated categories — Personal Development, Self Care, Diet, Lifestyle, Relationships — or build your own.

🎉

Celebration Moments

Optional celebration sounds and animations when you complete a habit or finish your entire day's list.
