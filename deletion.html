Your Privacy Rights

Request Data Deletion

You have the right to request deletion of your device data from our servers at any time. This page explains what data we hold, how to request deletion, and our commitment to process it within 30 days.

What We Store

Your habit data never leaves your device. All habits, streaks, statistics, and preferences are stored locally only. This deletion request covers only the anonymous technical data described below.

Device Installation Record

Installation ID A randomly generated unique identifier created when you first installed the app. Not linked to your name or any account.
FCM Token A Firebase Cloud Messaging token used to deliver push notifications and habit reminders to your device.
Device Info Your device manufacturer, model name, and Android OS version (e.g., "Samsung Galaxy S23", Android 14).
Country Derived from your device's region settings. Not your precise location or GPS data.
App Version The version of Routinic you have installed and the date it was first installed.

Deleting Local Habit Data

🗂️ Your habit data is stored only on your device

To delete your local habit data, streaks, statistics, and preferences — we have no access to this data and cannot delete it for you. You control it entirely.

1
Open Android Settings
2
Go to Apps → Routinic
3
Tap Storage → Clear data
4
Or simply uninstall the app — all local data is permanently removed

⚠️ This action is irreversible. Your habit history cannot be recovered once cleared.

Note: Uninstalling the app does not automatically delete your device installation record from our Firebase servers. Please submit the deletion request above if you also want that data removed.
